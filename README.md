# 🐉 Clashy Dragon

A fast-paced, **Clash Royale-inspired** take on the classic **Flappy Bird** formula. Fly your baby dragon through dangerous obstacles, collect fireballs from friendly minions, and blast your way to a new high score. With smooth gameplay, familiar Clash-inspired visuals, and an increasing level of challenge, Clashy Dragon puts a fresh spin on an arcade classic.

## ✨ Features

* 🐉 Play as a baby dragon inspired by Clash Royale.
* 🌲 Dodge procedurally generated log obstacles.
* 👾 Collect fireballs dropped by friendly minions.
* 🔥 Shoot obstacles to clear your path.
* 📈 Increasing difficulty as your score rises.
* 🎮 Simple, responsive controls that are easy to learn but difficult to master.
* 🌐 Play directly in your browser — no download required.
